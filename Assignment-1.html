Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my favourite food. Here is the recipe for delicious scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt Butter in a frying pan over a medium heat
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
  3. Once Butter had melted add cream and eggs
  4. Using a spatlia flid the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center for every 20 seconds
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove from the heat
